History Made

On 12th October 2019 in Vienna, Austria,
Eliud Kipchoge, INEOS, and Nike made history.
Eluid Kipchoge became the first person
to break two-hours in the marathon.

The Stage

INEOS is a British multinational
chemicals company who staged the event and assembled a team of experts from the world performance and athletics, including people across the wider INEOS sports group.
First Attempt

On May 6, 2017, Nike hosted an event on the Formula One Autodromo Nazionale Monza race track in Italy. Three runners Eluid Kipchoge, Lelisa Desisa, and Zersenay Tadese took part but failed in breaking two. Kipchoge won in 2:00:25-to much disappointment in the athletics world.





The Shoe


Since 2016, Nike collobrated with Kipchoge to identify the exact specifications necessary for runners to perform at peak efficiency.
As a result, the Nike ZoomX VaporFly Next% was designed which allows runners to achieve an energy return greater than 4%.
