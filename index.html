History Made

On 12th October 2019 in Vienna, Austria,
Eliud Kipchoge, INEOS, and Nike made history.
Eluid Kipchoge became the first person to achieve
the impossible by breaking sub-two-hour marathon
barrier, finishing in a remarkable time of 1:59:40.2.
INEOS is a British multinational chemicals company who staged the event and assembled a team of experts from the world of performance and athletics, including people across the wider INEOS sports group.
First Attempt
On May 6, 2017, Nike hosted an event held on the Formula One Autodromo Nazionale Monza race track in Italy. Three runners Eluid Kipchoge, Lelisa Desisa, and Zersenay Tadese took part but failed in breaking two. Kipchoge won in 2:00:25- to much dissappointment in the athletics world.

The Shoe

Since 2016, Nike collobrated with Kipchoge to identify the exact specifications necessary for runners to perform at peak efficiency. As a result, the Nike ZoomX VaporFly Next% was designed which allows runners to achieve an energy return greater than 4%
