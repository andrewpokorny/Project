Barriers Broken

On 12th October 2019 in Vienna, Austria, Eliud Kipchoge, INEOS, and Nike made history. Eluid Kipchoge became the first person to achieve the barrier-breaking sub-two-hour marathon, finishing in a remarkable time of 1:59:40.2.

The Shoe

Since 2016, Nike collobrated with Kipchoge to identify the exact specifications necessary for runners to perform at peak efficiency. As a result, the Nike ZoomX VaporFly Next% was designed which allows runners to achieve an energy return greater than 4%

INEOS

INEOS is a British multinational chemicals company that staged the event and assembled a team of experts from the world of performance and athletics, including from across the wider INEOS sports group.
